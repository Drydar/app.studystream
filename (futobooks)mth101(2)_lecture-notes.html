Indices

Let a be any real number. The number of times a multiplies itself is called the power of a
In general, if we express a in the form am, we call m the power or index of a
And a = base or radix

Rules of Indices

1. am x an = am + n
2. am ÷ an = am ─ n
3. (am)n = amn
4. a1/n = n√a
5. am/n = (n√a)m or n√am
6. a0 = 1
7. a─ n = 1 an
8. (ab)n = an bn
9. ( a b ) = am bm
